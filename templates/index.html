Monitor Blood Pressure

Regular BP monitoring can reduce heart attack risk by up to 50%. Aim for <120/80 mmHg.

🏃

Stay Active

Just 30 minutes of moderate exercise 5 days a week can significantly reduce cardiovascular risk.

🥗

Heart-Healthy Diet

A Mediterranean diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and omega-3 fatty acids protects your heart.

😴

Quality Sleep

7-9 hours of quality sleep per night helps regulate heart rate and blood pressure naturally.

🧘

Manage Stress

Chronic stress elevates cortisol levels, increasing heart disease risk. Try meditation or yoga.
